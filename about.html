Who we are

Welcome to Elite Retreat Spa and Therapy, located in the heart of Kennesaw, Georgia. Since our establishment in 2007, we have been dedicated to providing a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. As a proud local business, we prioritize personalized care and attention to detail, ensuring every client receives a rejuvenating experience tailored to their needs.

At Elite Retreat, we offer a range of services designed to promote relaxation, wellness, and healing. From therapeutic massages to luxurious spa treatments, our skilled therapists are committed to enhancing your overall well-being. We specialize not only in traditional massage techniques but also in holistic approaches that address both physical and mental health.

In addition to our spa services, Elite Retreat is proud to offer professional physical therapy services. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing chronic pain, or seeking to improve your mobility, our licensed therapists are here to support your journey to optimal health.
